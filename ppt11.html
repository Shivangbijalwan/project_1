What is Isckon



Welcome to ISKCON 🌍 - INTERNATIONAL SOCIETY FOR KRISHNA CONSCIOUSNESS 🙏 Founded in July 1966 by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) is a global spiritual movement 🌟 rooted in the rich traditions of the Gaudiya-Vaishnava sampradaya. Often referred to as the Hare Krishna movement, ISKCON is dedicated to spreading the timeless wisdom of Vedic culture 📜 through the practice of bhakti-yoga—devotion to the Supreme Lord, Sri Krishna 🕉️. 🌿 A Global Family United in Devotion 🌿 With over 400 temples 🛕, 40 rural communities 🌾, and more than 100 vegetarian restaurants 🍽️ worldwide, ISKCON is more than a society—it’s a global family 🫂 united by a shared purpose. From bustling cities 🌆 to tranquil villages 🏞️, our centers serve as havens of spiritual learning, worship, and community. Our humanitarian initiatives, such as the renowned "Food for Life" program 🍛, embody our commitment to compassion, offering free vegetarian meals to those in need, nourishing both body and soul 💛. 🌼 Our Sacred Mission: Spreading the Joy of Krishna Consciousness 🌼 ISKCON's mission is to illuminate the path of self-realization and God consciousness ✨ for people across the world. Our teachings emphasize the transformative power of the Holy Names: Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare Hare Rama Hare Rama Rama Rama Hare Hare In the age of Kali-yuga, this sacred mantra 🎶 is a direct channel to the Supreme Lord, bringing peace, joy 😌, and spiritual awakening to all who chant it. 📚 Our Spiritual Foundation: Wisdom from the Vedas 📚 At the core of ISKCON's teachings are the Vedic scriptures, including the Bhagavad-gita 📖 and Srimad-Bhagavatam 📜, which guide us in developing a personal, loving relationship with Sri Sri Radha Krishna. These texts are not merely historical or philosophical works; they are living guides 💡 to a life of devotion, received through an unbroken chain of spiritual teachers 👳‍♂️ in the Brahma-Madhva-Gaudiya Vaishnava sampradaya. This lineage connects us directly to the divine wisdom imparted by Lord Krishna Himself 🙌 and passed down through saints like Srila Vyasadeva, Srila Madhvacharya, and Sri Caitanya Mahaprabhu 🕉️. 🌸 Our Eternal Philosophy: Living the Principles of Sanatana Dharma 🌸 ISKCON's teachings transcend sectarian boundaries, embracing the universal principles of sanatana dharma—the eternal, intrinsic duty of every soul 🕊️. This duty is expressed through loving devotional service (bhakti-yoga) to the Supreme Personality of Godhead, Sri Krishna 💖. Whether through meditation 🧘‍♂️, community service 🤝, or the simple act of sharing a smile 😊, every moment is an opportunity to deepen our connection with the Divine and with each other 🌟. Join us on this sacred journey 🚶‍♂️ toward spiritual enlightenment, and experience the profound joy 🥰 of Krishna Consciousness. At ISKCON, you'll find not just a place of worship, but a welcoming community 🏠 dedicated to love, peace ✌️, and the eternal teachings of Lord Krishna 🙏.
