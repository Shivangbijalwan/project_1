What is Isckon



Welcome to ISKCON 🌍 - INTERNATIONAL SOCIETY FOR KRISHNA CONSCIOUSNESS 🙏

Founded in July 1966 by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Prabhupada, ISKCON is a global spiritual movement 🌟 rooted in the Gaudiya-Vaishnava traditions. We are dedicated to spreading the wisdom of Vedic culture 📜 through bhakti-yoga—devotion to Lord Krishna 🕉️.

🌿 A Global Family 🌿

With over 400 temples 🛕, 40 rural communities 🌾, and more than 100 vegetarian restaurants 🍽️ worldwide, ISKCON is a global family united by love and devotion. Our centers serve as spiritual havens, and our "Food for Life" program 🍛 offers free meals to those in need, nourishing both body and soul 💛.

🌼 Our Mission 🌼

ISKCON's mission is to spread the joy of Krishna Consciousness. The sacred mantra: Hare Krishna Hare Krishna Krishna Krishna Hare Hare, Hare Rama Hare Rama Rama Rama Hare Hare 🎶, brings peace, joy 😌, and spiritual awakening to all who chant it.

📚 Our Spiritual Foundation 📚

Our teachings are based on the Bhagavad-gita 📖 and Srimad-Bhagavatam 📜, which guide us to develop a loving relationship with Lord Krishna. These texts provide living guidance 💡 through the Brahma-Madhva-Gaudiya Vaishnava tradition 🕉️.

🌸 Our Philosophy 🌸

ISKCON embraces the universal principles of sanatana dharma—the eternal duty of every soul 🕊️ to serve Lord Krishna 💖 through bhakti-yoga. Every act, whether meditation 🧘‍♂️, community service 🤝, or a simple smile 😊, is an opportunity to connect with the Divine.

Join us on this sacred journey 🚶‍♂️ toward spiritual enlightenment, and experience the joy 🥰 of Krishna Consciousness at ISKCON, a place of worship and a community of love, peace ✌️, and devotion to Lord Krishna 🙏.
